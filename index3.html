What is Coding?

Coding is same as our language. Only difference is that our language is to communicate with other “organisms”, on the other hand the coding is a language for all the stuff in the electronic devises. All the programs, electronic devices, applications, and websites kinds of things are all made by coding.

Languages in coding

As in our world, there are several different types of languages, in coding world, there are several different types of languages as well. For example, there are C, C+, C++, unity, C#, Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, etc.

Origin of Coding

The first computer coding programing was invented in 1883 by Ada Lovelace. She worked together with Charles Babbage. They worked really hard on the really early computer. Many people said that it is even miraculous how they make the coding during 19th century.
